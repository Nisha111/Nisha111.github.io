India, 2013

In 2013, I went to visit India for my cousin's wedding. It was an amazing expereince! Not only did I get to attend the most grand wedding I have ever seen, I visited royal palaces in Jaipur, and toured Agra and the Taj Mahal. I got to spend time with family I had not seen in 12 years.

Cruises, 2012

Our cruise to the Caribbean was a lot of fun. We stopped at several islands including the Caymans, Bahamas, Key West, and Mexico. We went jet skiing, scuba diving, and relaxed on the beach. I highly recommend traveling with Royal Caribbean if you get a chance. The boat was luxurious and the food was delicious.

Malaysia, 2010

My trip to Malaysia was a once-in-a-lifetime experience with my mother and grandmother. I got to see the city where my mom grew up and met extended family I had never met before. We planned our trip during Chinese New Year and got to participate in authentic Chinese festivities in Penang and Singapore.
